How to use this app

Gmail Auto-Reply

Click "Process Unread" to sync your emails. For new senders, you'll see them in the Approval Center. You can allow them permanently or once.

AI Email Creator

Enter a recipient, subject, and a prompt. The AI will generate a high-quality draft that you can rewrite or send instantly.

Security

All AI processing is local using Ollama. Your data never leaves your machine except when sending an email via Gmail API.
